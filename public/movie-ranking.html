The Best Movies According To Yash

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Pirates Of The Caribbean

The script is clever;
Cinematography is fine;
Special effects are spot-on.
Pirates Of The Caribbean Curse Of The Black Pearl is a terrific adventure film with action, drama, suspense, some romance, and humor.
Johnny Depp is amazing as Captain Jack Sparrow and he's clearly having a lot of fun with this character.

Fight Club

"Fight Club" is the most frankly and cheerfully fascist big-star movie since "Death Wish," a celebration of violence in which the heroes write themselves a license to drink, smoke, screw and beat one another up.

Whiplash

What does it mean to be great? I loved Whiplash because Damien Chazelle highlights the blood, sweat and tears that come along with pure devotion to one’s craft; the hard work when nobody’s there to pat you on the shoulder. The final scene…wow.
